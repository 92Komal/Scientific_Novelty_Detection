The results of all shared tasks were announced at the conference, and these proceedings also include overview papers for the shared tasks, summarizing the results, as well as providing information about the data used and any procedures that were followed in conducting or scoring the tasks. In addition, there are short papers from each participating team that describe their underlying system in greater detail. Like in previous years, we have received a far larger number of submissions than we could accept for presentation. WMT 2019 has received 48 full research paper submissions (not counting withdrawn submissions). In total, WMT 2019 featured 12 full research paper oral presentations and 102 shared task poster presentations.
